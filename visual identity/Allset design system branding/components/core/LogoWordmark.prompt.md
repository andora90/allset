Allset Logo Wordmark — "allset" in Cormorant Garamond italic with a 2px Bark underline. Use `dark={true}` on Obsidian slides/sections.

```jsx
<LogoWordmark size="md" />                    {/* Obsidian text, light bg */}
<LogoWordmark size="md" dark={true} />        {/* Cream text, dark bg */}
<LogoWordmark size="sm" />                    {/* 32px — slide footer */}
<LogoWordmark size="lg" />                    {/* 72px — brand cover */}
<LogoWordmark size="xl" />                    {/* 96px — landing page hero */}
```

Sizes: `sm` (32px) | `md` (48px) | `lg` (72px) | `xl` (96px)
Always place on Obsidian or Cream — never on photographic backgrounds without overlay.
