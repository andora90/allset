Slide eyebrow label — uppercase DM Sans in Bark. Place above every slide title. Format: "01 — Section Name" or just the section name.

```jsx
<SlideEyebrow dark={true}>01 — Le Constat</SlideEyebrow>
<SlideEyebrow dark={false}>02 — La Solution</SlideEyebrow>
<SlideEyebrow dark={true}>Présentation investisseurs</SlideEyebrow>
```

`dark={true}` → slightly muted Bark (for Obsidian slide bg)
`dark={false}` → full Bark (for Cream slide bg)
